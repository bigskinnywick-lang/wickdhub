What is inForm Safety?

INFORM Safety (Information and Reports Manager) is a reporting and data visualization tool included with the PrePass bypass service, built by HELP Inc. It transforms complex FMCSA government data into user-friendly dashboards, helping motor carriers understand their ISS score (the metric that determines bypass eligibility), track violations, coach drivers, and maintain weigh station bypass privileges.

It pulls from 100+ data sources including CVIEW/SAFER carrier records — the most robust fleet safety database in the industry. Accessed through the PrePass customer portal alongside Equipment Management, Transponder Management, and Reports.

Core Features

Company Snapshot Dashboard
  • ISS Score with last updated date
  • Fleet Summary: vehicles, drivers, inspections, OOS rates
  • Inspections Summary: total inspections, violations, OOS events over 24 months
  • CSA BASICs bar chart showing all seven categories
  • 24-month trending pie chart (severity time weight)
Data Insights — Heat Maps & Drill-Downs
  • Color-coded heat maps showing violation intensity by geography (national, state, county, highway)
  • Google Maps/satellite integration — see inspection sites at street level
  • Filter controls: date range, violation type, category, state
  • Details tab: every inspection in last 24 months, sortable by site
  • Search by VIN number, site, or violation type for specific fleet trends
  • Export: local files, XML, or CSV
  • Subscriptions tab: recurring weekly/monthly email reports

The Seven BASICs

1 Unsafe Driving
2 Crash Indicator
3 Hours-of-Service (HOS)
4 Vehicle Maintenance
5 Controlled Substances/Alcohol
6 Hazardous Materials
7 Driver Fitness

CSA scores are calculated from roadside inspections, crash reports, and violations over a 24-month window. Lower = better. High scores lead to audits, higher insurance premiums, lost business with shippers, and delays at inspection sites.

Related Products Agents Should Know

INFORM Tolling

Separate product, same portal. Data analytics for managing PrePass Plus toll transactions. Features toll visibility across agencies, user-defined fraud alerts (high-volume charges, unassigned devices, restricted areas), and annual financial reporting comparing spend to cash toll rate.

PrePass Safety ALERTS

Real-time audible and visual notifications via the PrePass Mobile App. Categories: Parking (select states), Road Restrictions (brake checks, steep grades, truck ramps), Traffic (congestion, work zones), Weather (winter hazards, flooding, wind). Works nationwide, integrates with TSP apps.

Bypass Basics — Quick Reference

  • How it works: AVI identifies truck → valid credentials + good safety record → Weigh-in-Motion → green bypass signal
  • Must pull in if: red light on transponder, app says "Pull In," or no signal received
  • Random pull-ins: all fleets subject to them — maintains system integrity
  • Transponders: PrePass (top of windshield), Fusion/ElitePass (bottom), Mobile App (floor/dash/windshield)
  • Florida Ag Interdiction: non-ag carriers need PrePass Ag service to bypass
