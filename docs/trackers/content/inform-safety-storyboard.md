# inForm Safety — Storyboard (CS Audience)

Storyboard for the CS-facing inForm Safety module: product awareness, talk tracks, and the clean hand-off to Tech Support. 7 sections, 13 slides, ~10 minutes.

**Audience scope:** Customer Service agents. Focus is on recognizing the product, naming three features, and knowing when (and when not) to bring it up on a call. Setup, data reading, and troubleshooting belong to the companion Tech Support course.
