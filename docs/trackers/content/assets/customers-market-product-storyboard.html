Customer Journey — Interactive Walkthrough
LEARNER INTERACTION
PROCESS / LABELED GRAPHIC
Interaction Design

Rise Block: Process interaction (horizontal steps) or Labeled Graphic with numbered hotspots. Learner clicks through each step sequentially. Each step reveals a short description and a visual.

Concept: Follow “J&R Transport” — a mid-size fleet — through a complete PrePass experience in one day.

1
Approach — 1 Mile Out
The truck approaches a weigh station. The PrePass transponder (or MOTION mobile app) sends a signal about one mile before the station. The system checks the carrier’s safety record, registration, and compliance status in real time.
2
Decision — Bypass or Pull In
Within seconds, the driver gets a green light (bypass — keep rolling) or a red light (pull in for inspection). Green means the carrier’s safety profile passed. This decision happens automatically, every time, at every equipped station.
3
Bypass — Rolling Through
The driver passes the weigh station at highway speed. No stopping, no idling, no inspection line. Multiply this by dozens of stations on a cross-country route and the time savings become massive.
4
Toll Plaza — Seamless Payment
Same device, different function. The PrePass transponder doubles as an electronic toll tag. As the truck passes through a toll plaza, the toll is captured and billed automatically. One device handles both bypass and tolling — no separate E-ZPass or SunPass needed.
5
Compliance Check — Behind the Scenes
While the driver rolls on, FleetDrive 360 is working in the background. The fleet’s compliance status — FMCSA safety ratings, DOT registration, insurance filings — is continuously monitored. If something changes, the carrier gets an alert before it becomes a roadside problem.
6
Back Office — Billing & Insights
At the end of the day, the fleet manager reviews everything through the Carrier Portal: bypass transactions, toll charges, compliance alerts, and safety analytics. One platform, one invoice, complete visibility. NEEDS PRODUCT INPUT — Confirm portal/dashboard details with Vedrana/Hannah.

Core Service: Weigh Station Bypass
CONFIRMED CONTENT
TEXT + IMAGE BLOCK
On-Screen Text

Weigh Station Bypass — The service that started it all.

PrePass electronically screens commercial vehicles approaching weigh stations and inspection sites. Carriers with strong safety records and valid credentials receive a green light to bypass — no stopping required. The system uses RFID transponders (mounted to the windshield) or the MOTION mobile app to communicate between the truck and the station.

How it works: State agencies set the screening criteria. PrePass checks the carrier’s safety score, registration, insurance, and size/weight credentials in real time — about one mile before the station. If everything checks out, the driver gets a green light. If not, they pull in for a standard inspection.

Voiceover Script

Weigh station bypass is where PrePass began, and it’s still the foundation of everything we do. Here’s how it works: when a truck with a PrePass transponder or the MOTION app approaches a weigh station, the system checks that carrier’s safety record, registration, insurance, and credentials — all in real time, about a mile before the station. If the carrier meets the state’s screening criteria, the driver gets a green light and keeps rolling. If not, they pull in. It’s fast, it’s automatic, and it happens at over five hundred eighty-four locations across the country.
