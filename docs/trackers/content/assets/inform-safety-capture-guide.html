Capture Instructions

All live captures need to come from the inForm Safety portal (accessed through the PrePass customer portal under Reports). Use a demo or test account with realistic data if possible — we need populated dashboards, not empty states.

Resolution: Capture at 1920×1080 minimum. Use full-page screenshots where possible. Annotate key areas with numbered callouts after capture.

Naming convention: inForm_[number]_[description].png (e.g., inForm_01_portal_nav.png)

Save to: inForm Safety/inForm_captures/
