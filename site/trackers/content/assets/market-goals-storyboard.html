Strategic Priority
Platform Expansion
Compliance Integration
Geographic Expansion
Data & AI Innovation
Business Reason
Carriers prefer one vendor over five — a unified platform increases retention and lifetime value
Adding regulatory services to the bypass/toll ecosystem makes PrePass harder to replace
Every new state strengthens the network and widens the competitive moat
Millions of daily data points can become new services that carriers will pay for

Correct matches: Platform ↔ one vendor, Compliance ↔ harder to replace, Geographic ↔ network & competitive moat, Data ↔ new services from data points.

Feedback (correct): “That’s right. Each priority connects to a clear business reason — they’re not abstract goals, they’re strategic moves with purpose.”

Feedback (incorrect): “Not quite. Review the priorities above and think about what each one achieves for the company. Try again.”

Builder Note

This matching activity directly tests Learning Objective 1 (“Identify PrePass’s four strategic priorities and describe what each one means”). The “business reason” phrasing is Awareness-level — learners recognize the connection, they don’t need to generate it from memory. Rise “Knowledge Check — Matching” block is the ideal format. If matching isn’t available in the Rise plan, a multiple-select (“select all that apply”) or sorting activity can substitute.
